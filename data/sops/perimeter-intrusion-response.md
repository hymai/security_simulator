# SOP: Perimeter Intrusion Response

Applies when the Perimeter Intrusion Detection System (PIDS) raises an alarm on
the Zone 3 fence line, or a fence breach is otherwise reported.

## Step 1 — Alarm verification and initial assessment
- **SCC Operator**: Verify the perimeter intrusion alarm against the CCTV camera
  covering the Zone 3 fence line. Confirm whether it is a genuine breach or a
  false activation (wildlife, weather) before escalating.
- **SCC Operator**: Announce the alarm activation over radio to all Security
  Officers on duty, stating the zone.
- **Security Officer**: Proceed to the Zone 3 fence line and visually confirm the
  breach.
- **Security Officer**: Report observations — number of intruders and direction
  of travel — back to the SCC.

## Step 2 — Containment and coordination
- **SCC Operator**: Track the intruder on CCTV and direct responding officers.
- **Security Officer**: Maintain a safe standoff distance; do not attempt to
  physically detain unless directed and safe to do so.
- **SCC Operator**: Escalate to the Duty Manager and, if a threat to persons or
  critical assets is assessed, request Police assistance.

## Step 3 — Resolution and reporting
- **Duty Manager**: Decide on resolution (eviction, hold for Police, or safe
  withdrawal) based on officer reports.
- **SCC Operator**: Log all timings and actions in the incident record.
- **Duty Manager**: Complete the post-incident report and notify the site
  manager.
