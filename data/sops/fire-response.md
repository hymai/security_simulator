# SOP: Fire Response and Evacuation (Block B)

Applies when Block B smoke or heat detectors activate, or a fire is reported in
Block B (control room and offices).

## Step 1 — Alarm, notification and evacuation
- **SCC Operator**: Activate the building fire alarm and notify the fire service.
- **Fire Warden**: Initiate evacuation of Block B to the designated assembly
  point.
- **Duty Manager**: Account for all personnel at the assembly point using the
  attendance roster.
- **Security Officer**: Secure the perimeter gate to allow emergency vehicle
  access.

## Step 2 — Support to emergency services
- **Security Officer**: Meet the fire service at the Zone 1 gate and escort
  appliances to Block B.
- **SCC Operator**: Provide the fire service with the building fire plan and
  confirm the personnel roll-call status from the Duty Manager.

## Step 3 — All-clear and reporting
- **Duty Manager**: Do not permit re-entry until the fire service declares the
  building safe.
- **SCC Operator**: Record the incident timeline and reset the fire panel once
  authorized.
- **Duty Manager**: File the fire incident report.
