# SOP: Tailgating / Unauthorized Access Response

Applies when an access-control door on Block A or Block B is forced or an
unauthorized person is reported following an authorized cardholder inside.

## Step 1 — Detection and challenge
- **SCC Operator**: Review the door-controller alarm against the CCTV camera at
  the affected door and confirm an unauthorized entry.
- **SCC Operator**: Direct the nearest Security Officer to the affected door.
- **Security Officer**: Locate and politely challenge the unauthorized person,
  requesting identification and their reason for access.

## Step 2 — Containment
- **Security Officer**: Escort the person to reception and prevent further
  movement into controlled areas.
- **SCC Operator**: Verify with the host or cardholder whether the person is
  expected.

## Step 3 — Resolution and reporting
- **Duty Manager**: Decide whether to grant supervised access, evict, or hold for
  Police.
- **SCC Operator**: Record the event and the access-control log in the incident
  record.
