# Marina Vista Tower — building profile and fire-safety systems

Marina Vista Tower is a 30-storey Grade A office tower in Singapore's downtown
core with a 3-storey F&B and retail podium and 4 basement carpark levels.
Weekday occupancy is around 4,000 people across some 60 tenant companies.
The building holds a Fire Certificate, has an appointed Fire Safety Manager
(FSM), and maintains a Company Emergency Response Team (CERT) led by a Site
Main Controller (SMC) and Site Incident Controller (SIC).

## Key locations

- **Fire Command Centre (FCC)** — level 1, behind the main lobby; houses the
  main fire alarm panel, PA/EVAC system console, CCTV wall, and the lift
  supervisory panel. Manned by security 24/7.
- **Main lobby** — level 1, four speed-gate lanes, visitor management counter.
- **Loading bay** — basement 1, shared dock for tenant deliveries; chemical
  deliveries for the podium F&B outlets and cleaning contractors pass here.
- **Chiller plant** — basement 2; two water-cooled chillers using R-134a
  refrigerant; plant room is card-access controlled.
- **Electrical risers** — stacked beside the service lift core on every floor.
- **Refuse chute and bin centre** — basement 1, adjacent to the loading bay.
- **Assembly area** — the open-air plaza across Straits View Lane, 80 m from
  the tower's main entrance, marked with assembly point signage.

## Fire-safety and security systems

- Addressable fire alarm system with smoke detectors on every floor,
  reporting to the FCC main panel; alarms also auto-transmit to SCDF.
- Full sprinkler coverage; wet risers in both exit staircases; hose reels on
  every floor.
- PA/EVAC voice communication system with floor-by-floor zoning for phased
  evacuation announcements.
- Two pressurised exit staircases; firefighting lift on the service core.
- Smoke control: carpark ventilation runs an extraction mode; office floors
  use the AHUs, which the FCC can shut down per floor to stop smoke spread.
- CCTV coverage of the lobby, lift lobbies, carpark, loading bay and refuse
  areas, recorded and viewable from the FCC.
- Standby generator (basement 2) supplying essential services: exit lighting,
  fire pumps, one lift per bank, FCC systems. Rated for 6 hours on the day
  tank.
- Lift bank supervisory panel in the FCC can recall all lifts to level 1.
