# SOP: Fire response and evacuation

Applies to any confirmed or suspected fire in Marina Vista Tower. Executes
the evacuation component of the building's Emergency Response Plan (ERP).

## Stage 1 — Alarm verification

1. **FCC Security Officer**: On any fire alarm activation, identify the
   device address on the main fire alarm panel and check the corresponding
   CCTV camera. Do not silence the alarm before verification.
2. **FCC Security Officer**: Dispatch the nearest Security Officer or CERT
   responder to the device location to verify by sight, and inform the Fire
   Safety Manager and Site Main Controller immediately.
3. **Security Officer (on scene)**: Report back to the FCC by radio: fire
   confirmed or false alarm, exact location, size, and whether anyone is in
   the vicinity. Do not attempt to fight a fire larger than an incipient
   (wastepaper-basket size) stage.

## Stage 2 — Confirmed fire

4. **FCC Security Officer**: Call SCDF at 995, state the building address,
   fire floor and what is burning. The alarm auto-transmits, but the voice
   call must still be made.
5. **Site Main Controller**: Activate the CERT over the radio and take
   command at the Fire Command Centre. Account for which CERT members are on
   site and assign the Site Incident Controller to the incident floor.
6. **Site Incident Controller**: Proceed to the incident floor with the CERT
   response team via the firefighting lift or stairs (never a normal lift),
   confirm the fire situation, and direct first-aid firefighting with hose
   reels only if it is safe to do so.
7. **FCC Security Officer**: Shut down the AHU serving the fire floor to
   stop smoke spread, and recall all lifts in the affected bank to level 1
   using the lift supervisory panel.

## Stage 3 — Evacuation

8. **Fire Safety Manager**: Order phased evacuation over the PA/EVAC system:
   fire floor first, then the floor above and the floor below, then the rest
   of the building as directed. Announce the use of exit staircases only.
9. **CERT response team members**: Sweep their assigned floors — check
   toilets, pantries and meeting rooms — and direct occupants into the
   nearest pressurised exit staircase. Assist persons with mobility
   difficulties to the staircase landing and report their location to the
   SIC for SCDF's attention.
10. **Security Officers (ground)**: Keep the driveway and fire engine
    access clear, open the gate barriers, and meet arriving SCDF appliances
    to hand over the FCC keys and fire floor information.
11. **Site Main Controller**: On SCDF arrival, hand over command to the SCDF
    ground commander and provide the fire floor, systems status, and the
    location of any persons awaiting assistance.

## Stage 4 — Assembly and accounting

12. **Fire Safety Manager**: At the assembly area (the open-air plaza across
    Straits View Lane), collect headcount reports from each tenant's fire
    warden against their attendance registers.
13. **Fire Safety Manager**: Report any unaccounted-for persons and their
    last known location to the SCDF ground commander immediately.
14. **Site Main Controller**: Permit re-entry only after SCDF declares the
    building safe, and announce re-entry over the PA system.
